# Live FX

## Live Effects

Various audio effects allow you to change the overall sound. As soon as the menu is exited, the master effects go off.

 The Snap-Back function is activated by default and returns the effect controls to the center when released.

 If the Hold button in the center is activated, the effects remain on.

 Master effects are suitable for transitions between Patterns, which is why the fade time is also set in this menu.

## Auto-Switch Length

Length of the crossfade between the patches when autoplay is enabled.

## Pattern Switch Length

Length of the crossfade between the patter when manual crossfade is enabled.

## Echo & Time

Radius (X): Echo Volume

 Angle (Y): Echo Time

 At a low angle, the echo time is long, as the wing increases, it becomes shorter.

 The Echo & Time controller is directly connected to the Echo EQ Decay, which controls the equalizer and the length of the echo.

## Echo EQ Decay

Radius (X): Echo Length/ Feedback

 Angle (Y): Filter EQ

 This control only works in combination with the Echo & Time Controller.

 Tip: The effect can be well understood when Hold is active and the controller is moved in the angle.

## Reverb & Filter

Radius (X): Reverb Volume

 Angle (Y): Filter EQ

 The filter shifts depending on the angle

 The Reverb Time & Decay controller associated with the controller affects how long the reverb is.

 The reverb fades out when the Reverb& Filter controller is centered again or FX menu is exited.

## Reverb Time & Decay

Radius (X): reverb strength

 Angle (Y): reverb decay time

 At a low angle, the reverb is short; as the angle increases, the reverb becomes longer.

 This control only works in combination with the Reverb & Filter Controller.

## Reverse Sound

Radius (X): Strength of the effect

 Angle (Y): Filter, Pitch, Reverse mode.

 The Reverse effect takes the last few seconds of the master sound currently playing and plays it backwards. The effect is combined with filter and pitch settings via the angle.

## Flanger

Radius (X): Flanger Strength

 Angle (Y): Style of the flanger effect.

 The flanger shifts the stereo signal by milliseconds, resulting in a fluctuation of the pitch.

## Hold effects

This function is for preventing the snap back behaviour of the effect send controllers.

 By exiting the FX Interface, this function gets deactivated automatically.

## Dynamic Envelope Length

Radius (X): Effect Strength

 Angle (Y): Envelope length of all sequencer notes becomes temporarily longer

 Starting from the 0° position at 9 o'clock, all sequencer notes are played shorter or longer in proportion.

## Dynamic Grain Distance

Radius (X): Attack Fade

 Angle (Y): Release Fade

 Dynamically affects the duration and length of all granular Samplers in the Pattern. This means that when off, the attack and decay/release are at maximum, so the fade is soft when the controller is off.
