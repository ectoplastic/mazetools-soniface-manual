# Terms of use

## Terms of use

An app license allows the owner to commercially use the compositions, sounds and images created in this app for the own artistic, musical or educational purposes. We explicitly welcome this and appreciate any mentions under the hashtag #Mazetools.

 For companies with intentions beyond these use cases, please send a collaboration request support@mazetools.com. We look forward to your requests and posts!

### Permissions

Soniface requires your permission to access your device's hardware for certain functionality. No personal information is collected.

 All hardware access permissions are for interaction and user experience and are explained in more detail below.

### Camera & Microphone

Camera

 Soniface uses access to your device's camera(s) for motion tracking via webcam. This way you can control the modules with your body movements.

 Microphone

 To record sounds into Soniface via the microphone, the app needs permission to the microphone of your device.

### Network & User Folder

Network

 Soniface requires access to the network for the Ableton Link Interface. This allows you to synchronize the musical tempo with other apps.

 User folder

 To save and load sessions, presets, recordings, and microphone recordings, Soniface needs access to the file system and creates its own folder.

### Power & Permissions

Sound and visuals are generated in real time. We continuously optimize performance, but we recommend a power supply.

 We recommend turning off the visuals if you want to save power or improve performance.
