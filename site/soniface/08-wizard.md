# Wizard

## Add new instrument...

Each instrument offers versatile settings and presets: Gridsynth soundscapes and Sampler loops, Granular sound structures, Rhythms through Drums and Bass.

 Select a category and double tap to add a instrument. This creates a Maze item on the screen and in the list.

## Add LivePad Module

The LivePad controller allows you to record an instrument directly on the Grid surface.

 Every interaction is looped, allowing you to quickly create soundscapes, melodies and Rhythms.

## Add Live Gridsynth

The Gridsynth is controlled by the LivePad Controller and played directly on the Grid surface.

 Tap twice to add the instrument.

## Add Live Sampler (Melodic)

The pitch of the Sampler is controlled by the LivePad controller and played directly on the Grid surface.

 Tap twice to add the instrument.

## Add Live Drums

The drums (kick, snare, hi-hat) are controlled by the LivePad controller and played directly on the Grid area.

## Add Live Bass

## Add Live Grain Time

A Granular Sampler harmony is controlled by the LivePad controller and played directly on the Grid surface.

 Tap twice to add the instrument.

## Add Gridsynth Module...

The Gridsynth is the heart of Soniface: the combination of Grid visual and synthesizer.

 The tones are spread on the Grid and there are different ways to play the Grid...

## Add Gridsynth

The Gridsynth is played as a held note and tonally changed by the Maze position.

 Tonal changes are created via the Editor or Multi Touch.

 Tap twice to add the instrument.

## Add Sample Gridsynth

The Gridsynth is combined with a sample and played as a held tone. The Maze Position determines the tonal change.

 Sound changes are created via the Editor or Multi Touch.

 Tap twice to add the instrument.

## Add Sampler Modul...

The Sampler includes a granular synth, a sample loop function for soundscapes and a melodic pitch shifter for melodies.

 In granular mode, samples are extremely stretched.

 In harmonic granular mode, soundscapes and Arpeggiator melodies are tonally matched to scale/chord in the Chord Interface.

## Add Granular Instrument

The Granular Sampler allows for different soundscapes and musical textures.

 Granular Synthesis divides a sample into tiny fragments (grains). The temporal progression is manipulated by the length and spacing of the grains.

 In Hamonize mode, the tonality is aligned with the scale/chord in the Chord Interface.

## Add Preset...

Choose between several presets that serve as a starting point for a new creation.

 Soniface differs here from the Soniface Pro version: while in the regular version only 5 Mazes are available, in the Pro version you can add as many new Mazes as you like.

 Note that the previous settings are overwritten when presets are activated in an instrument that is already in use.
